What Do Cats Eat?

Simply said, cats are carnivores.For a robust heart, good vision, and a healthy reproductive system, they must consume meat protein. They can get it by eating cooked beef, chicken, turkey, fish, and modest portions of lean deli meats.

In the wild, cats eat a variety of small prey, so mixing up the protein sources in your cat's food can tap into their instincts and satisfy their craviings.
