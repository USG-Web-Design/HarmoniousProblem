Chihuly Garden and Glass

Dale Chihuly may be the Pacific Northwest’s most famous glass artist. His fanciful glass creations have been exhibited at more than 200 museums, gardens and other venues around the world.
Closer to home, the Seattle area celebrates the Tacoma-born artist’s work at Chihuly Garden and Glass, where radiant red and gleaming gold “plants” sparkle in the outdoor gardens, and several indoor galleries showcase Chihuly’s dramatic glass inventions.
